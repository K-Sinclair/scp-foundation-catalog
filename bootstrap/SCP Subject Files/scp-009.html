SCP-009: The Red Ice

Item #: SCP-009

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

Object is to be contained within a sealed storage tank of heat-resistant alloy with dimensions not less than 2m x 2m x 2m...

Description:

SCP-009 is approximately ███ liters of a substance which superficially resembles distilled water (H2O), except with a distinct bright red hue...

Addendum: Circumstances of Retrieval

Subject was found in ████, Alaska, on 11/05/19██. The Foundation became involved after reports were obtained from the native ████ Tribe...

Addendum: Testing Log

Testing on D-Class personnel was discontinued as of 4/23/20██ due to unforeseen consequences...
